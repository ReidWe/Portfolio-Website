About
Who I am
Drag the badge
I'm an Electrical Engineering student who thrives at the boundary between the physical and the digital. I started with breadboards and soldering irons, and over time grew into PCB design, embedded firmware, and signal processing.

I love the challenge of taking a concept through every stage — from napkin sketch to schematic, from layout to fabrication, from first power-on to working prototype. Whether it's analog, digital, or a mix of both, I'm always looking for the next thing to build.
